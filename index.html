Welcome to dice!

A couple of rules before we begin...

    The game has 2 players, playing in rounds.

    In each round, a player can roll the dice as many times as they wish.
    Each roll is added up towards the player's current score.

    BUT, if a player rolls a 1, all of their score for that round is lost.
    After that, it's the next player's turn.

    Also, if a player rolls (2) 6's in a row, they lose both
    the current round and their total score.

    A player can choose to 'Hold' at any time, which will add their
    current round score towards their total score.
    After that, it's the next player's turn.

    The first player to reach the winning score, wins the game.
